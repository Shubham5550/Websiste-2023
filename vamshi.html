About


MyVrends is a mobile app whoch aims to bring students together by making it easy for students to meet new friends. It is difficult for students to connect and build a friendly network when they are new to a college. It is hard to adjust to a new culture while trying to enjoy the many things the college has to offer.

According to a survey of approx 48000 college students from madison University in 2017. 64% reported feeting extremely lonely in the last year. So why waste your college life by being alone? Instead of ultra-complex. MyVrends focuses on helping you to build new network of true friends by

  • Providing an easy to use platform for organizing activities and
  • Joining fun & exciting events to meet people of similar interests
By design, MyVrends is simple and easy to navigate and for you to explore different events around you. If you want to meet new people, socialize with locals with similer interests, expand your social network, from study groups or join a pickup soccer game, you've come to the right the place.

MyVrends is built by a group of students, alums and faculty from northeastern University. We are committed to change network building from simply online to more offline in-person interactions. If you are planning to join an event in college campus and are feeling shy, then MyVrends will help you to find someone you know to go with you. So Sign up to MyVrends mobile app, register and organize an event and post it. It's that simple!

Why MyVrends?


MyVrends focuses on connecting like-minded people and helping them to do things they enjoy! We differentiate ourselves from other social networking apps in being dedicated to solely offline networking by

  • Offering an intutive user interface
  • Enabling you to easily browse or host offline events
  • Allowing incoming students to find & meet new friends, study partners and roommates
  • Provide an easy referral program based on QR codes
  • Rewarding those who refer their friends,MyVrends "Booster" points to gain "Social Influencer" status and enjoy benefits!
